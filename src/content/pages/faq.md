headerTagline: Got Questions?
faqs:
  - question: Are you ISO certified?
    answer: Yes, we are ISO 9001:2015 certified. The certification can be found on https://www.pcbdirectory.com/manufacturer/profile/valleytek-inc
  - question: What manufacturing standards do you build to?
    answer:  We build products to IPC-A-610 Class 2 and Class 3 workmanship standards.
  - question: Can you handle medium and high volume production runs?
    answer: Yes, we have offshore partners for lower cost options for mid to high-volume production. 
  - question: What PCB sizes and component types can you handle?
    answer: We can assemble boards from 0.3" × 0.3" up to 20" × 20". We are capable of placing 0201 chip sizes and fine-pitch components, as well as BGAs and other complex package types.
  - question: How fast can you build and deliver my order?
    answer: Depending on your needs, we can support turnaround times from a standard 10-day turn all the way down to a 1-day expedite. Contact us to discuss your specific schedule requirements.
  - question: What information do you need to provide a PCB assembly quote?
    answer: >-
      To generate an accurate quote, please provide:

      - Bill of Materials (BOM)

      - Assembly Drawing (top and bottom)

      - Gerber files

      - General assembly standard requirements (e.g. IPC-A-610 Class 3)

      - Any special requirements

      - Desired lead time

      You can submit all of this via our contact form.
  - question: Do you offer consignment, turn-key, and partial turn-key options?
    answer: Yes. We offer full consignment (you supply all materials), full turn-key (we source all materials), and partial turn-key (a combination). We can discuss what works best for your project.
  - question: Do you support both leaded and lead-free (RoHS) assembly?
    answer: Yes. We support both standard leaded and lead-free RoHS-compliant assembly processes. Please specify your requirement when requesting a quote.

Valleytek provides comprehensive PCB assembly services using state-of-the-art surface mount technology (SMT) and through-hole assembly lines. From quick-turn prototypes to high-volume production runs, our experienced team delivers precision assemblies with rigorous quality control at every step.

**Assembly types:**
- Surface Mount Technology (SMT) — including fine-pitch and 0201 chip sizes
- Through-Hole Technology (THT)
- Ball Grid Array (BGA)
- Mixed technology (SMT + through-hole)

**Specifications:**
- Board sizes from 0.3" × 0.3" up to 20" × 20"
- Lead-free (RoHS) and standard leaded processes
- Consignment, turn-key, and partial turn-key options
- Turnaround from 10-day standard to 1-day expedite

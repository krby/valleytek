Our skilled technicians perform precision PCB rework and repair on assemblies of all complexity levels. Whether you need component replacement, solder joint correction, or BGA reballing, we restore your boards to full specification with the same quality standards applied to new assemblies.

**Capabilities include:**
- Through-hole and SMT component removal and replacement
- BGA rework and reballing
- Fine-pitch IC rework
- Trace repair and pad restoration
- Conformal coating removal and reapplication
- IPC-A-610 Class 2 and Class 3 workmanship standards
